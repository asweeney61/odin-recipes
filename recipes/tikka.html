Chickpea Tikka Masala

Description

Chickpea Tikka Masala is an incredibly easy and absolutely divine vegan version of the classic Indian dish! It tastes deliciously indulgent, yet its still healthy. Plus, vegan tikka masala makes large portions that are perfect for meal prep!
